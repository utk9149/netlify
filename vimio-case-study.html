Vimio: 130% Sales Growth Through Targeted Client Acquisition

Client Overview: Vimio is a fast-growing tech company focused on providing digital solutions in the media and entertainment sector.

Challenges

Vimio faced challenges in scaling its client acquisition efforts due to:

  • An unfocused client acquisition strategy, leading to inefficient sales processes.
  • The sales team struggled to close high-value deals, resulting in missed revenue opportunities.
  • Limited market research, leading to poor targeting of potential clients.

Solutions Implemented

Honchi worked closely with Vimio to implement the following solutions:

  • Targeted Client Acquisition Strategy: We developed a strategy that focused on high-value clients, streamlining the sales process to minimize inefficiencies.
  • Sales Funnel Optimization: We optimized their sales funnel, improving the client experience from initial contact to deal closure.
  • Sales Team Training: We trained the sales team on advanced sales techniques and tools, empowering them to close more high-value deals.

Results

  • Vimio achieved a 130% increase in overall sales, with significant growth in high-value client acquisitions.
  • Client conversion rates improved by 40% thanks to the optimized sales funnel and targeted strategies.
  • The sales team was able to close deals 20% faster, leading to faster revenue realization and higher profitability.
