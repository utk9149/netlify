Emids: Achieving 150% Sales Growth with Honchi

Client Overview: Emids is a leading provider of digital transformation solutions for healthcare payers, providers, and life sciences companies.

Challenges

Emids faced several challenges in achieving consistent sales growth:

  • They had an inefficient customer acquisition funnel, which led to high lead drop-offs and poor conversion rates.
  • Lack of market segmentation made it difficult to focus on high-value opportunities.
  • The sales team required better training and alignment with the company's strategic goals.

Solutions Implemented

Honchi devised a comprehensive strategy to address these challenges:

  • Sales Strategy Development: We segmented Emids' target market and identified high-growth opportunities. A new sales strategy was created to focus on those segments.
  • Customer Acquisition Funnel Optimization: We redesigned the customer journey, focusing on lead nurturing and increasing engagement at critical stages of the funnel.
  • Sales Team Training: We provided customized training to equip the sales team with the skills needed to close high-value deals efficiently.

Results

  • Emids experienced a 150% increase in sales within 12 months due to the implementation of the new strategy.
  • The optimized acquisition funnel led to a 30% increase in lead-to-client conversions, significantly improving their overall revenue growth.
  • Sales team productivity improved by 25%, allowing them to handle more clients and close deals faster.
